All About Our Game & Team:

While there are many versions of Bubble-Burst, no one (including Wikipedia) seems to know who invented it the game itself. However, it is derived from another popular game called Bust-A-Move, which belongs to the genre of 3-Match Games where one must put like things together to get rid of them and progress.

We are a team of students at Epicodus Coding Bootcamp. Our names are Anna Kuznetsova, Anna Timofeyeva, Michael Dunlap, and Mick Sexton.
